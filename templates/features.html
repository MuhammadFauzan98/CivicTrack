Powerful Features For Smarter Civic Management

CivicTrack gives citizens and government teams a clear, fast, and transparent workflow for reporting, managing, and resolving urban issues.

Smart Location Mapping

Interactive maps with clustering, heatmaps, and geolocation help teams quickly identify issue hotspots and prioritize action.

Complaint Filing With Images

Citizens can file complaints with detailed descriptions, location details, and photo evidence for faster verification and response.

Government Workflow Dashboard

Officials can assign issues, track statuses, set priorities, and monitor department performance from one centralized dashboard.

Analytics And Reports

Live charts, trends, and export-ready reports provide actionable insights for planning, accountability, and service improvement.

Secure Role-Based Access

Authentication and role-based permissions ensure that each user sees and manages only the functions relevant to their responsibility.
