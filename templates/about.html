About CivicTrack

Track. Resolve. Improve.

Our Mission

CivicTrack is dedicated to bridging the gap between citizens and government officials by providing a transparent, efficient platform for reporting and managing urban issues. Our mission is to create smarter, more responsive cities through technology and data-driven decision making.

What We Do

  • Enable citizens to report urban issues quickly and easily
  • Provide government officials with powerful tools for complaint management
  • Use AI to categorize and prioritize issues automatically
  • Offer real-time tracking and transparency
  • Generate insights through data analytics

How It Works

1. Register

Create your free account as a citizen or government official.

2. Report

Submit complaints with photos, location, and detailed descriptions.

3. Track

Monitor the status of your complaints in real-time.

4. Resolve

Government officials work to resolve issues efficiently.

Key Features

AI-Powered

Automatic categorization and prioritization of complaints using machine learning.

Interactive Maps

Visualize complaints on maps with heatmap visualization and geolocation.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards with charts, trends, and performance metrics.

Why Choose CivicTrack?

  • Transparency: Track every complaint from submission to resolution
  • Efficiency: AI-powered categorization saves time and resources
  • Accountability: Clear assignment and tracking of responsibilities
  • Data-Driven: Make informed decisions with comprehensive analytics
  • User-Friendly: Simple interface for both citizens and officials
  • Responsive: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
